About

Long Story Short

My love for user experience design stems my desire to create things that make someone's day better in a small or large way. It all started when I was a little dude enthralled by my favorite cartoons and obsessed with creating my own characters with traditional drawing mediums. And then one day... my parents bought the familyâ€™s first home computer. I loved playing on it and wondered how the UI and interactions were made. I thought it would be amazing to create them. Little did I know these two interests would lead to learning design and finding clients in need of small branding projects.

By senior year of college, I was learning some basic front end development and found designing basic static posters subpar. Finally, upon graduation I decided to go after the career I've been searching for since my obsession started that joins my interests in design, psychology, and technology: user experience and interface design.

Education

I graduated from Seton Hill University in May 2017 with a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration (Marketing Specialization), a Graphic Design Certificate, and a Psychology minor.

Since then I have been taking courses on Javascript.
